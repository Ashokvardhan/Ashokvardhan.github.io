Movies

As you might already know by this point, I am an avid film watcher. While I initially started with the Hollywood movies, running out of options I slowly shifted my attention to foreign language ones. This habit started from my undergraduate days at IIT Bombay. While it started as a means to merely do some time pass, with time I began to appreciate movies as a means of communication that filmmakers use to convey a spectrum of messages and make the audience think and introspect themselves even after one leaves the cinema theatre. In particular, I like the movies that explore themes that are very central to human behavior the most. The following is a list of some of the best movies across multiple languages that I have watched till date which might be of use for any potential movie lover like me. Please let me know if you are interested in watching any of these (probably not gonna happen :P) which I gathered with some effort.
